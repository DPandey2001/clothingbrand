Our Story

A journey of passion, craftsmanship, and timeless elegance

Founded on Excellence

@Honey - Bae was born from a vision to redefine luxury fashion. Our founder, inspired by the timeless elegance of European couture and the innovation of contemporary design, set out to create a brand that would bridge the gap between tradition and modernity.

Every piece in our collection tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship, premium materials, and attention to detail that sets us apart in the world of luxury fashion.

Our Journey

2025

The Beginning

@Honey - Bae was founded with a vision to create timeless, luxury fashion pieces that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design sensibilities.

2025

First Collection

Launched our debut collection featuring handcrafted silk dresses and cashmere accessories, immediately gaining recognition from fashion critics worldwide.

2025

Global Expansion

Opened our first flagship stores in Paris, Milan, and New York, bringing our vision of accessible luxury to fashion capitals around the world.

2030

Sustainable Future

Committed to sustainable fashion practices, introducing eco-friendly materials and ethical production methods without compromising on quality or style.

2035

Innovation Continues

Launching our revolutionary 3D virtual fitting experience and expanding our digital presence to serve customers worldwide with personalized luxury shopping.
